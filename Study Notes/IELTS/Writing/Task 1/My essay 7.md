### üìñ Vocabulary Breakdown & Definitions

As requested, here are the definitions for the technical and high-level words used:

- **Facilitates (Verb):** To make an action or process easy or easier.
    
    - _Usage:_ "Trees **facilitate** the absorption of water into the ground."
        
- **Disrupt (Verb):** To interrupt an event, activity, or process by causing a disturbance or problem.
    
- **Precipitation (Noun):** Rain, snow, sleet, or hail that falls to the ground. (The professional term for "rain").
    
- **Transpiration (Noun):** The process where plants absorb water through their roots and then give off water vapor through their leaves.
    
- **Permeable (Adjective):** Allowing liquids or gases to pass through it.
    
    - _Usage:_ "Sand is more **permeable** than concrete."
        
- **Infiltration (Noun):** The process by which water on the ground surface enters the soil.
    
- **Baseflow (Noun):** The portion of streamflow that comes from "underground" water (groundwater) rather than direct rain runoff.
    
- **Curtailed (Verb):** To reduce in extent or quantity; to impose a restriction on.
    
    - _Usage:_ "Urbanization has **curtailed** the natural water cycle."
        
- **Surge (Noun/Verb):** A sudden powerful forward or upward movement.

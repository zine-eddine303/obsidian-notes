# My essay

The pie chart illustrates ~~the~~ ~~==student's==~~ students' average monthly spending in the United Kingdom in year 2016, while the bar chart represents the sources of the income of students in the same year.

Overall, housing takes as much as all what the other expenses combined take in the monthly spending of students. Furthermore, most source of income for these individuals is though student loans, while grants and fundings are the least common means of income.

Students spend 385 pounds for leasing a place to live in, this figure represents half of what they spend per month. Food is directly adjacent with 124 pounds spent, followed by leasure activities such as socialising and traveling which cost 64 and 52 pounds respectively. Bills are one important expense that costs students 69 pounds on average each month. Finally, a 91 pounds is dedicated to some other undefined expenses.

More than 80% of students have student loans as one of their sources of funds, 70% receive money from their parents and family members and also from working part time shifts; however, around 40% use their savings and bank overdrafts. Grants and fundings are the least common ways of earning incom among students.

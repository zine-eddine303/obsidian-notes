In many countries, the tradition of families eating meals together is disappearing. Why is this happening? (**Problem**) What are the possible solutions? (**Solution**)

### "Causes and Solutions" vs. "Causes and Effects"

- **Causes and Solutions:** You identify why a problem exists and how to fix it.   
- **Causes and Effects:** You identify why a problem exists and what the **consequences** are.
 

> **Warning:** If you provide solutions when the prompt only asked for "effects," you lose marks for Task Response. Read the last sentence of the prompt twice!

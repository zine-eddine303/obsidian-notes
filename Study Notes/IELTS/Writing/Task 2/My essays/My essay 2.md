Obesity is a worldwide health issue that is spreading among young individuals. This essay believes that the main causes for this health hazard are the overconsumption of unhealthy food and the excessive inactivity of these children and suggests that their parents and their governments should monitor their food habits and encourage them to be more active.

Not practicing sports and consuming junk food are one of the main reasons of obesity. Nowadays kids spend most of their time at home playing video games, watching television or scrolling through social media and all these activities are require sitting still for long periods of time; hence, no energy consumed and this leads to obesity. Further more, the latter individuals eat fast food and sweets as breakfast, lunch and diner which adds up to the fat storage in their bodies, and thus, becoming overweight.

the bar chart shows the time per week male and female youth spend on exercise and sports in England.

Overall, males are more active than females. Males spend more time exercising and playing sports in comparison to females. In addition, the number of females who don't spend any time in such activities are the double of their male counterpart.

Over 40% of females spend no time excercising in their week, while more than 15% exercise at least an hour per week. One fifth of females play sports between 1 to 3 hours per week. 1 of 10 females spend between 5 and 7 hours per week training. Less than 5% play sports from more than 5 hours per week.

Almost a quarter of males don't play any sports throughout their week. The percentage of males who spend less than an hour, between 3 and 5 hours, and between 5 to 7 hours per week is around 10% for each range. 20% of males spend 1 to 3 hours per week training.
